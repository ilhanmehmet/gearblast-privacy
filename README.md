Privacy Policy for Gear Blast

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Introduction
Welcome to Gear Blast. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you play our Android mobile game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your experience complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other global privacy standards.

2. Data Collection and Usage
Gear Blast is designed to prioritize your privacy. We do not require account registration on our servers, nor do we collect personally identifiable information directly.

Local Device Data: To provide a seamless puzzle experience, game progress, profile names, avatars, scores, playstyle badges, league points, and gameplay optimization metrics are stored locally on your device. This data is processed locally by our in-game systems to adjust gameplay dynamics and is never transmitted to or stored on our external servers.

Third-Party Services: We use essential third-party services to operate the game, provide leaderboards, and serve advertisements. These services may collect standardized device identifiers (such as IP addresses and Advertising IDs) as permitted by their respective privacy policies.
